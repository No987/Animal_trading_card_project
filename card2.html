Giant Panda

Giant Pandas are a bear species native to China. They are distinguished by a distinct black and white coat and rotund body.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda Melanoleuca
  • Average Height: 2-3 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Live mainly in temperate forests high in the mountains of southwest China

The giant panda has a diet almost exclusively of bamboo. On average the giant panda eats as much as 20 to 30 pounds of bamboo shoots a day.
